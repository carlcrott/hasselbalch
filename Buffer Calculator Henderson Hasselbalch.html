Buffer Calculator
This easy online calculator helps you make buffered soltuions at a given pH and concentration.

Did you know -- adjusting your pH by adding NaOH or HCl releases an undefined amount of salt in your buffer.  This increases your solution's ionic strength, which can inhibit enzymes or cause precipitations.  The best way create buffers of defined ionic strength is to use the equation of Henderson and Hasselbalch.  That's what this easy calculator does for you.
